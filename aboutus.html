History of SR Group

It all began with the inspiration of one man, Shri. Varada Reddy in 1976. His vision was as clear then as it is now...to impart value-based quality education to shape young minds.
"Students must be made to feel that all our efforts are for their benefit in the future."
This driving force has enabled the SR Group to carve a niche for itself in the field of education today. His foresight in providing equal opportunities to every student irrespective of their social or economic backgrounds has made him an icon for others to emulate.
Setting benchmarks and spreading the SR success story by opening up portals of education at various levels, the Group has set up landmark institutions in various states.
Right from giving children a solid foundation base in school to carrying them forward into college and getting them job-ready with the required skills to start their careers, SR has led the way.
Encouraging children to discover, get inspired and shape out a meaningful future, learning at an SR institution means a lot more than just classroom education. It embraces various crucial elements from learning to environment and approach to methodology.
The SR approach to education has been instrumental in shaping students into disciplined, dignified and highly focused individuals motivated to be responsible citizens of the country.
The SR Group leaves no stone unturned in providing the right environment and facilities to enable a student to blossom. Stimulating curiosity in young minds and encouraging a child to think creatively is encouraged and imbibed along with timeless values coupled with a contemporary approach to take education to a new level.
